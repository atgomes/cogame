Deuterium Costs for Flights:
In system:
1 + ROUND[fuel * ((1 + 0.005 * (Number of planets)) / 35) * (% / 100 + 1) ^ 2]
Between Systems:
1 + ROUND[fuel * ((2.7 + 0.095 * (Number of systems)) / 35) * (% / 100 + 1) ^ 2]
Between Galaxies:
1 + ROUND[fuel * ((20 * (Number of galaxies)) / 35) * (% / 100 + 1) ^ 2]
